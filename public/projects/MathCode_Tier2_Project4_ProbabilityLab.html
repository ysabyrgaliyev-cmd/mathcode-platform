How probability works in code: Instead of calculating probability with formulas, we can simulate thousands of experiments using random. The code flips a coin or rolls dice over and over, counting the results. As you run more trials, the experimental probability gets closer and closer to the theoretical probability. This is called the Law of Large Numbers. Try it!
